About Me

Welcome to my world of design and innovation.

My journey

I am Aries Filiam I am a multidisciplinary creative with a background in graphic design, web development, and digital systems. My work focuses on building visual identities and digital experiences that combine structure with artistic expression. I draw inspiration from pop art, bold visual language, and artists such as Banksy, Jean-Michel Basquiat, and Malika Favre — creators who transform simplicity into powerful communication. My approach blends strategy with aesthetics. Every project I develop aims to communicate clearly, stand out visually, and create meaningful impact.

I believe in clean design, intentional aesthetics, and ideas that speak without shouting.

Minimal form. Maximum meaning.
